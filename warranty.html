Warranty Policy

Effective date: August 11, 2025

Lifetime Workmanship Warranty

We warrant our installation workmanship for as long as you own the vehicle. If a leak or issue is caused by our installation, we will repair it at no cost.

What’s Not Covered

  • New damage caused by impacts, abuse, or accidents after installation.
  • Rust, corrosion, prior body damage, or previous adhesive affecting adhesion.
  • Manufacturer defects in glass or parts (covered by manufacturer warranty).
  • ADAS/camera recalibration (not provided by us).
  • Chip repairs that later spread; appearance can vary and is not guaranteed.

How to Make a Warranty Claim

  1. Contact us at ccglassrepair@gmail.com or (503) 949-2257.
  2. Provide your name, vehicle, service date, and photos if possible.
  3. We’ll schedule an inspection and resolve covered workmanship issues.

This warranty gives you specific legal rights; you may have other rights which vary by state.
